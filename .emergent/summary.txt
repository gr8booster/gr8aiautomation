<product_requirements>
The goal is to transform GR8 AI into a market-ready SaaS platform that scans websites, recommends/deploys AI automations (chatbots, lead capture, scheduling, marketing sequences), and includes a fully automated AI Automation Report lead magnet system. The platform needs to attract, qualify, and convert website visitors into leads and paying users, and ultimately offer an AI Workforce Scan feature.

Key features include:
*   **Authentication**: Initially Google OAuth + demo mode, transitioned to email/password login.
*   **Website Analysis**: AI-powered scanning (GPT-4o-mini) to generate automation recommendations and a comprehensive AI Automation Report (Lead Magnet).
*   **AI Automations**:
    *   **AI Chatbot**: Embeddable widget for real-time AI responses.
    *   **Lead Capture**: Embeddable forms with AI scoring (hot/warm/cold) and personalized auto-responses.
    *   **Appointment Scheduler**: Backend for managing availability and bookings.
    *   **AI Content Generator**: For blog posts, product descriptions, marketing copy.
    *   **AI Email Assistant**: For drafting replies and campaigns.
    *   **Social Media Scheduler**: For automated posting.
    *   **Visual Workflow Builder**: For creating custom automation workflows.
*   **Monetization**: Stripe billing with Free/Starter/Pro tiers; lead magnet reports are free to view on-screen, but email delivery and PDF download are gated behind payment/subscription.
*   **Analytics Dashboard**: Charts and metrics for usage, performance, and lead reports.
*   **Lead Magnet System**: Dedicated Free AI Automation Audit landing page, progress bar, visual report preview, SendGrid email nurture sequence (3 emails) for follow-up, CRM tagging. PDF reports are generated but stored locally or in-memory.
*   **AI Workforce Scan (GR8 Workforce Intelligence)**: Extends website scan to analyze job postings (Careers, Jobs) to recommend AI agents for roles, estimate cost savings, and generate a unified report with Human vs AI Workforce Composition chart.
*   **Platform Integration**: Widgets accessible from the main app, setup wizards for automations, marketplace for templates, team workspaces, SMS notifications (Twilio), CRM integration options.
*   **Branding & UI**: Professional, polished UI with specific fonts (Space Grotesk, Figtree) and a primary teal color, responsive design, custom hero image with GR8 AI AUTOMATION text, robots, and connectivity.
*   **Production Readiness**: Removal of demo data, configuration of real API keys (SendGrid, Stripe, Twilio, Sentry), production domain (gr8booster.com), SSL, database cleanup, security hardening, robust email automation, monitoring, and comprehensive user onboarding.

The user frequently emphasized that features *must* be visible on the UI and *actually* work from their perspective, even when screenshots or internal tests suggested otherwise.
